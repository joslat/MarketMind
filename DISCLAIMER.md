# Disclaimer — not investment advice

**MarketMind is a research & education project.** It models **exposure** and explained impact paths through
a curated company-dependency graph — it does **not** predict prices and is **not** investment advice.

On a held-out test of 27 events it showed **no demonstrated next-day directional edge** — it did not beat a
whole-sector baseline (~47% directional accuracy on the held-out TEST split, within noise of chance). Its
validated value is **reach** (surfacing non-headline movers the headline never named) and an **auditable
reasoning path** — not a trading signal.

**Do not use MarketMind, on its own, to make buy or sell decisions.** Nothing here is a recommendation to buy,
sell, or hold any security. Past behaviour of the model on historical events does not indicate future results.

Provided **AS-IS, with no warranty and no liability**. If you act on anything produced by this software, you do
so entirely at your own risk. Consult a licensed financial professional before making investment decisions.
